Our Story

A Dream Stitched Into Every Frock

Shainy Creation was founded by Shainy Mathew in Bengaluru, India, with a single belief: every girl deserves to feel beautiful, celebrated, and magical. What started as handmade frocks for family and friends quickly grew into one of India's most trusted children's fashion brands.

Today, our team of 40+ artisans creates over 500 unique designs each year, using only the finest fabrics sourced from across India and abroad. Every frock is quality-checked three times before it reaches your doorstep.
