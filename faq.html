Orders & Shipping

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for select pincodes at an extra charge during checkout.
Is Cash on Delivery available?
Yes! COD is available across India for orders up to ₹5,000. A small COD handling fee of ₹49 applies.
Is there free shipping?
Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹1,500. Orders below this have a flat shipping charge of ₹99.
Can I track my order?
Yes! Visit our Track Order page and enter your Order ID. You'll get step-by-step tracking from packing to your doorstep.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship across India only. International shipping is coming soon — sign up for our newsletter to be notified.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unused, unwashed items in original packaging. Custom and sale items are non-returnable.
How do I initiate a return?
Email us at hello@shainycreation.com with your order ID and reason for return. Our team will arrange a pickup within 48 hours.
When will I get my refund?
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned item is received and quality-checked by our team.

Sizing & Products

How do I choose the right size?
Our sizes are age-based. Refer to the size guide on each product page. When between sizes, always size up for comfort and room to grow.
Are the colours accurate in photos?
We make every effort to ensure accurate colour representation. Slight variations may occur due to screen settings and fabric dye batches.
Can I customise a frock?
Yes for bulk orders of 10+ pieces. Contact us with your requirements and our design team will assist you.
What fabrics do you use?
We use premium organza, satin, net, crepe, and cotton blend fabrics — all skin-tested for children's use and free from harmful chemicals.
